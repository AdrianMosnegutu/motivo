\chapter{Design of the Motivo Language}\label{ch:language-design}

This chapter specifies Motivo at version~2.0: hierarchical structure, grammar, types, temporal semantics, and
compilation strategy.
Version~1.0 is mentioned only where the v1$\to$v2 evolution clarifies a design choice.
Key-signature declarations are not part of the current language; tempo and time signature are supported.
