\section{Use Cases}\label{sec:use-cases}

Motivo suits composers who reason about repetition and variation but lack a concise editable source form.
Typical workflows include offline batch composition (compile once, import the \texttt{.mid} into any host),
compact symbolic descriptions that expand into large event lists, teaching algorithmic composition with
immediate diagnostics and visualization in Motivo Studio, and rapid prototyping of motifs before manual
polish in a digital audio workstation.
