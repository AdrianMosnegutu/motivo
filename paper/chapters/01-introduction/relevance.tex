\section{Relevance}\label{sec:relevance}

Submitted as a bachelor thesis in software engineering, this work delivers a typed language, a validating
compiler, a browser-based studio, and the testing and deployment infrastructure to treat the result as a
maintainable product---not a one-off prototype. Deterministic MIDI output makes compilation reproducible and
the artefact importable into any standard host, so Motivo complements rather than replaces established
digital audio workstation workflows.
