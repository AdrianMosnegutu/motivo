\chapter{Background}\label{ch:background}

This chapter provides the foundational context necessary to understand the design and implementation of the
proposed domain-specific language.
It introduces core concepts from music theory, explains the technical details of the MIDI protocol, outlines
the fundamental stages of compiler construction, and explores the paradigm of algorithmic composition.
