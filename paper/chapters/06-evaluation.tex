\chapter{Empirical Evaluation}\label{ch:evaluation}

This chapter tests whether Motivo's structural compression works in practice: can compact symbolic source expand
deterministically into much larger MIDI timelines without a playback-time runtime?
The evidence covers source-to-event expansion (Section~\ref{sec:compression}), compile time on a local benchmark corpus
(Section~\ref{sec:compile-time}), and implementation limits (Section~\ref{sec:limits}).
No user study was conducted; musical quality and learnability are out of scope.
