\chapter{Evaluation}\label{ch:evaluation}

This chapter evaluates the proposed DSL compiler through multiple lenses: its functional capabilities, performance metrics, and the utility of its supporting tools.
The evaluation is divided into a quantitative analysis of the compiler's performance on complex musical programs, followed by an analysis of the expressiveness of the language and a comparative study against existing music programming tools.
