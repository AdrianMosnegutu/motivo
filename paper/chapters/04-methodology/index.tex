\chapter{Methodology}\label{ch:methodology}

This chapter outlines the theoretical framework and design principles used to develop the proposed domain-specific language.
It describes the mapping between musical theory and functional programming, the temporal model used for composition, and the rationale behind the compiled architecture.
Unlike systems designed for live coding and real-time playback, this methodology prioritizes structural integrity, reusability, and deterministic output.
